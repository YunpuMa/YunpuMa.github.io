I am a Postdoc at the University of Munich, working with [Prof. Volker Tresp](https://www.dbs.ifi.lmu.de/~tresp/) and [Prof. Thomas Seidl](https://www.dbs.ifi.lmu.de/cms/personen/professoren/seidl/index.html). Additionally, I am a research scientist at Siemens, specializing in quantum machine learning for combinatorial optimization problems. Before Siemens, I spent three years as an AI researcher at LMU, where I earned my Ph.D., focusing on temporal knowledge graphs. 


My research interests encompass structured data learning, versatile multi-modal models, and quantum machine learning. I have authored eight primary papers and contributed to 30 co-authored papers in conferences like ICLR, NISP, AAAI, and EMNLP. My ultimate research goal is to advance general AI.
